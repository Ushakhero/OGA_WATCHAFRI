# 🤝 OGA_WATCHAFRI — Responsible AI Statement

## Our Commitment

OGA_WATCHAFRI is built with responsible AI principles at its core.
We are committed to protecting African users in a way that is safe,
transparent, fair, and respectful of their dignity.

---

## Privacy & Data Protection

- **No data storage:** User inputs are processed in real-time and never logged, stored, or retained
- **No personal data collection:** We do not collect names, phone numbers, account details, or any personally identifiable information
- **Session-based only:** Each conversation is independent — nothing carries over between sessions
- **No third-party sharing:** User reports are never shared with advertisers, data brokers, or third parties

---

## Transparency

- **Explains its reasoning:** Every detection result includes a clear explanation of why something was flagged
- **Shows confidence levels:** The agent clearly states HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW confidence — never pretends to be certain
- **Acknowledges limitations:** The agent openly states when it is unsure and recommends professional verification

---

## Limitations & Boundaries

OGA_WATCHAFRI is an AI assistant and has the following limitations:

- ⚠️ **Not a law enforcement tool** — it cannot investigate crimes, arrest individuals, or recover lost funds
- ⚠️ **Not a substitute for legal advice** — users should consult lawyers for serious cases
- ⚠️ **Not infallible** — it may miss novel fraud techniques or produce incorrect analysis
- ⚠️ **Not a bank** — it cannot freeze accounts, reverse transactions, or contact your bank on your behalf
- ⚠️ **English-primary** — currently optimized for English; performance may vary in local languages

---

## Fairness & Inclusion

- Designed for users of all education levels — language is kept simple and jargon-free
- Does not discriminate based on gender, ethnicity, religion, or location
- Covers fraud patterns across multiple African countries, not just Nigeria
- Designed to be accessible without requiring technical knowledge

---

## Deference to Human Authority

OGA_WATCHAFRI always defers to:
- Law enforcement (EFCC, Police, Interpol) for criminal investigation
- Financial institutions (CBN, Banks) for account security and fund recovery
- Legal professionals for advice on pursuing cases in court
- Medical/mental health professionals if a victim shows signs of distress

---

## Human Oversight

This system is designed to **assist human decision-making, not replace it.**
Every recommendation includes a call to action that involves a human authority
(call your bank, report to EFCC, contact police). The agent never acts autonomously
on behalf of the user.

---

## Misuse Prevention

OGA_WATCHAFRI is designed exclusively for fraud detection and victim support.
It is not designed to and will not:
- Help anyone commit fraud
- Provide information useful for scammers
- Identify or expose potential victims
- Be weaponized against the communities it serves
